# Remote Eligibility Notes

- Confirm whether the role accepts applicants working from Australia before applying.
- Check whether the employer can legally hire in Australia, use an employer-of-record, or pay contractors directly.
- Treat global/remote wording as a starting point, not as proof of eligibility.
- For U.S.-oriented roles, confirm whether U.S. citizenship, U.S. residency, or U.S. payroll is required.
- Prefer postings that clearly mention worldwide, APAC, Oceania, or Australia-friendly timezone overlap.
